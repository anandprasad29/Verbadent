No Data Collection

Verbident is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account creation or login required

  • No location tracking
  • No analytics or usage tracking
  • No cookies
  • No data sharing with third parties

Children's Privacy

Verbident is designed to be safe for users of all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no children's data to protect. Our app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.

Text-to-Speech

Our app includes a text-to-speech feature that processes text entirely on your device. No audio or text data is transmitted to external servers.

Local Storage

The app may store your preferences (such as language settings and custom sequences) locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the app.
